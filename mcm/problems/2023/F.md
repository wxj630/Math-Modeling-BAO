# 题目与问题：ICM-F: Green GDP

## 每问/小问拆解

| 编号 | 小问/任务 | 适合模型 | 说明 |
|---|---|---|---|
| Q1 | Select one already-developed way to calculate GGDP that could measurably affect climate mitigation if adopted as the main economic-health measure. | 环境会计、指标体系 | 选择 World Bank adjusted-savings 风格公式，避免自造不可审计指标。 |
| Q2 | Build a simple, defensible model for expected global climate-mitigation impact if GGDP replaces GDP. | 公开数据面板、确定性情景模型 | 使用 World Bank WDI 的 GDP、CO2 damage、资源耗减、森林耗减等公开指标。 |
| Q3 | Decide whether the global switch is worthwhile by comparing climate upside with replacement effort/downside. | 成本收益分析、多情景评价 | 比较 cautious pilot、phased G20/resource exporters、full switch 三种情景。 |
| Q4 | Choose one country and analyze how GGDP would change natural-resource use/saving and future-generation support. | 国家案例、多指标解释 | 选择 Brazil，显式关联 GDP 与 GGDP 计算差异。 |
| Q5 | Write a one-page non-technical report to the leaders of that country on support/reject. | 政策备忘录、非技术摘要 | 将模型结果转换为面向领导人的决策建议。 |

## 官方题面参数

- GDP 衡量一定时期内一国最终产品和服务的货币价值，但不惩罚资源消耗和环境后果。
- Green GDP 要纳入环境与可持续性视角。
- 题面要求选择一种已经提出过的 GGDP 计算方法，而不是重新发明不可审计口径。
- 需要估计如果 GGDP 被世界认可为主要经济健康指标，可能发生什么变化及其环境影响。
- 需要比较全球气候缓解收益和替代现状指标的阻力/代价。
- 需要选择一个国家做深入分析，并写一页非技术报告给该国领导人。

## 英文原文节选

Could a “Green” GDP (GGDP), where “Green” refers to the inclusion of environmental and sustainability perspectives and factors, be a better measure than the current conventional GDP? Your task is to consider the world recognizing GGDP as the primary measure of the health of a nation's economy. What changes should we expect? What would the environmental impact of those changes be?
