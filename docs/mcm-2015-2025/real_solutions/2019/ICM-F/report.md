## Policy Recommendation
Policy recommendation for national leaders: support a universal decentralized digital currency only through phased pilots, regulated settlement rails, privacy-preserving compliance, and audited stability mechanisms. Do not require immediate abandonment of national currencies. Countries with high unbanked shares and unstable currency conditions should start with wallet access and remittance corridors; high-trust banking economies should focus on oversight, custody, and interoperability.
