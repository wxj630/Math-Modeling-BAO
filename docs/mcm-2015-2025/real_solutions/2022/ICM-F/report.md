## UN Memo
Memo to the UN committee considering an Outer Space Treaty update

The official 2022 ICM-F question asks whether asteroid mining can still benefit all humankind. Our answer is yes, but only if the sector is licensed before exclusive norms harden. The recommended vision is un_licensed_benefit_sharing_regime: public and private mining may proceed, but under UN licensing, benefit sharing, open mission data, and risk bonding. The top sensitivity result is benefit_fund_share; the strongest downside is exclusive_property_claims. The first policy to implement is global benefit-sharing fund, followed by a global benefit-sharing fund and technology access pool. Without these rules, asteroid mining is likely to concentrate profits and voice among first movers; with them, the sector can improve participation, technology diffusion, and peaceful use while remaining technically feasible.
